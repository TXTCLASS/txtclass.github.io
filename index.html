About Zach

~ The Cake Was a Lie ~

But

since you're already here, you might as well get to know me a little better. I have a passion for all things digital, which has allowed me to develop my copywriting and marketing skills to suit the online world. I love to find creative solutions to problems, whether it be writing a catchy headline or creating content for a landing page.


I love to learn, in fact the more obscure or downright weird it is, the more engaged I'll be! I call myself a "Zach" of all trades, as I'm constantly absorbing information that may or may not be useful. That being said, my skill set makes me best suited to content strategy, copywriting, and graphic and web design.


If you want to get more personal... I like biking, Ethereum, writing, photography and creating unique graphics with Photoshop and Illustrator. Most of my creations are Toronto related, such as a Love Letter to the SRT or a TTC parody map which was actually reported on by the Torontoist and Global News!


Did I mention I know Esperanto? In fact, currently I'm working on a writing project called "Kvindek Du Semajnoj de Esperanto" or "52 Weeks of Esperanto". It's exactly what it sounds like, one blog post in Esperanto each week for a year... Fun!
