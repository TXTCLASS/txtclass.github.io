Overview

I've been on YouTube since 2007. My time on there has mostly been about learning new things or sharing some of the weird stuff I've made over the years. But that all changed when I started making videos again in September of 2016.

 

Before my videos were just uploaded right after shooting. With my new series called "SFP" or Short Film Projects, this is no longer the case. Each video is scripted, narrated, and edited by me on a topic I found interesting at the time. I try to mix in some light comedy with each one, but the end result is always a video I feel proud to have produced.
